Filling Out Your Bracket

Before Round 1 tips off, submit: every series winner (Round 1 through Finals), every series length (in 4, 5, 6, or 7 games), three MVP picks (East Conf Finals, West Conf Finals, NBA Finals MVP), and a tiebreaker — combined total points scored in Game 1 of the NBA Finals.

Once Round 1 starts, picks are locked. No changes allowed.
